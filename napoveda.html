Help – 🎮 Drunk Punk

What’s this game?
Simple farming strategy with low graphics, but big humor.
How do I get more money?
Sell your products at the market or in the pub. Every item has its price. Don’t be afraid to mess around!
Can I earn in other ways?
Hell yeah! You can sell stuff one by one, but pub & market quests pay better 😋
How do I brew beer?
You need a beer vat and boiler, 🌾 wheat, 🍃 hops and ⏳ time. First ferment, then boil. Each beer needs its own brew.
How do I make wine?
You need a wine press and wine vat, 🍇 grapes and ⏳ time. First press the grapes, then ferment into wine.
How do I distill spirits?
You need a ⚗ distiller, fruit and ⏳ time. First ferment the fruit, then distill it. Every shot starts with a brew.
How does the JZD work?
Buy animals with cash. Every beast eats 🌾 1 wheat per hour or it dies. And yeah, they poop 💩. Clean the crap or bye-bye cow! Use PI tokens to freeze food use for 24h.
What’s the deep fryer for?
Turn 🥔 potatoes into 🍟 fries. Just buy the fryer and go full grease mode.
What’s coming next?
Cafeteria, 🧀🥛 Dairy, 🍞🥨 Bakery. Ice cream, jam and more madness!
What’s the goal?
Chill out and enjoy! TAKE IT EASY!!!
Why does this game exist?
Because it’s fun, period 😇
What if something’s broken?
Try reloading. If it’s still messed up, write the dev. Or just grab a 🥃 shot and hit it again.
